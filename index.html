Welcome to Dungeons & Dragons

What is Dungeons & Dragons?

Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) is a tabletop role-playing game where players create characters and embark on adventures in a fantasy world.

The game is led by a Dungeon Master (DM) who describes the world and situations to the players. Players roleplay their characters and make decisions that influence the course of the story.

A Brief History

D&D was created in 1974 by Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson. It is the first tabletop role-playing game and it has inspired many other games of this genre.

D&D has had several editions over the years. The latest version, D&D 5e, was released in 2014.

How to Play

To play D&D, you need:

  • One or more players
  • A Dungeon Master (DM)
  • Dice
  • Character sheets
  • A rulebook

The DM can use a screen to hide their notes and dice rolls from the players.

Why Play D&D?

D&D is a fun and social game that allows you to:

  • Use your imagination
  • Have extraordinary adventures
  • Meet new people
  • Relax and have fun
